EMV Chip Card Solutions

what the EMV chip card is, how it works

EMV chip cards are a type of payment system card that utilize microchips to securely store and process data. Each time an EMV chip card is used, a unique code is generated for the transaction, providing a higher level of security compared to traditional magnetic stripe cards. This technology is part of the EMV security standard for payment systems, which helps to prevent fraudulent activity and protect sensitive financial information.

Benefits

Have a higher level of security
Cards with an EMV chip are processed faster
Used for a wider range of payments
Protection against online shopping fraud

services with EMV chip cards

1
Card issuance and activation: Our payment system provides a card issuance and activation service for EMV chip cards to card issuers. This includes technical support, chip tuning technologies, and mechanisms for personal identification and card activation
2
Transaction processing: PSiGate offers transaction processing services for EMV chip card transactions to banks. This includes card authentication, checking for sufficient funds, and verifying transaction limits
3
Fraud protection: Our payment system offers technology to protect EMV chip cards from fraud, including multi-factor authentication, transaction data protection, and prevention of counterfeit card fraud. This service is available to banks and other card issuers
