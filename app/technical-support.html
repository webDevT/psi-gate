Technical Support

Refund policy and quality assurance

Refund terms and conditions

Time limits for refunds

The refund period depends on the type of payment and the payment method used. Generally, refunds can be issued within 14 days of the payment being made. However, in some cases, additional time may be required to process the refund request.

Return procedures

To obtain a refund, please contact our technical support team with a description of the problem and relevant documents (e.g. screenshots). We will review your refund request as soon as possible and provide you with a response.

Return criteria

In order for a refund request to be approved, the payment must have been made within our system and it must also meet our refund criteria. Refund criteria may include, for example, an incorrectly made payment, an incorrect payment amount or double debit.

Quality assurance

Security

We process all payments in accordance with high security standards using advanced data encryption technology. We pay particular attention to protecting the confidentiality of user data and preventing unauthorised access to it. Our certificates of compliance with security and data protection standards confirm our commitment to data security and privacy.

Convenience

We strive to provide our users with maximum convenience when using our payment system. Our system easily integrates with various payment methods and allows fast and convenient payments anywhere in the world.

Reliability

We are constantly working to ensure the stability of our system and high performance. We strive to ensure that our users are able to make payments without disruptions or delays. In the event of technical problems, we are ready to respond quickly and fix them.
