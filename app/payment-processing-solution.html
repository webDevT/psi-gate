How do I register and start using the system?

Go to the payment system website.

Click on the "Register" or "Register" button.

Fill in all the mandatory fields in the registration form, including e-mail address and password.

Confirm the registration by following the instructions that will be sent to the specified e-mail address.

Login to your profile using the e-mail address and password provided during registration.

Add necessary details, such as bank cards or bank accounts, if required for using the payment system.
