We are launching a new feature

14.04.2023

The payment system is introducing a new feature that will allow users to set up automatic balance replenishment. With this feature, users can specify a particular amount to be automatically added to their account balance at regular intervals, eliminating the need to manually top up their accounts every time they need to make a purchase or payment.

This feature will be particularly useful for users who frequently make payments or have recurring bills that need to be paid regularly. By setting up automatic balance replenishment, users can ensure that they always have sufficient funds in their account, without having to worry about monitoring their balance or topping up their account manually.

The payment system will likely provide users with a range of options for setting up automatic balance replenishment, including the ability to specify the amount, frequency, and duration of the automatic top-ups. The feature is expected to be simple and user-friendly, with clear instructions and prompts to guide users through the setup process.

Overall, the introduction of automatic balance replenishment is a significant enhancement to the payment system's service offerings, providing users with greater convenience and peace of mind when it comes to managing their account balances.
