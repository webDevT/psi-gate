Point-of-Sale Systems

How to use the payment system

1
Browse the products or services you wish to buy on the merchant's website or app and add them to your shopping basket
2
Proceed to the checkout page and select a payment method. You can usually choose from options such as credit cards, debit cards, e-wallets, or bank transfers. If you are unsure which option to choose, you can contact the payment system's support team or the merchant for assistance
3
Enter your payment details. Depending on the payment method you select, you may need to provide information such as your card number, expiration date, CVV code, and/or bank address. Be sure to double-check your information for accuracy before submitting
4
Confirm the payment. Click the "Pay" or "Confirm" button to authorize the transaction
5
Wait for the payment to be processed. This usually takes only a few seconds but may take longer depending on the payment method you selected
6
Receive the product or service. Once your payment has been processed and confirmed, the merchant will send you the goods or provide access to the service. If you encounter any issues with the transaction, contact the payment system's support team or the merchant for assistance

If you have a problem with your payment or have not received the product or service, contact payment assistance or the merchant to resolve the problem
