Healthcare

We cater to the healthcare sector, an integral part of the economy, recognizing that healthcare services and well-being are paramount to individuals. Our primary goal is to simplify, streamline, and secure the payment process for healthcare services, ensuring utmost convenience for all our customers.

At PSiGate, we specialize in a comprehensive range of electronic payment system services, specifically designed to alleviate the financial burden on patients and facilitate seamless payment for healthcare services. Our online platform offers secure and user-friendly payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and mobile payments, enabling patients to conveniently settle medical expenses without the need to visit offices or endure lengthy queues.

Moreover, we provide automation and optimization services to healthcare payment processes, such as automated debits and payment notification systems. These solutions empower healthcare providers to focus on delivering exceptional healthcare services rather than managing intricate financial processes.

Respecting the importance of safeguarding patient privacy, we prioritize the security and protection of personal data during payment processing. Our practices adhere to stringent personal data protection legislation and adhere to the principles of GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation).

Our commitment to excellence is exemplified by our knowledgeable and dedicated team, well-versed in healthcare intricacies, coupled with a robust and resilient infrastructure. This ensures the seamless operation of our payment systems 24/7. We continually invest in the development and enhancement of our services, striving to deliver a superior customer experience and satisfaction for all healthcare payment requirements.

Why you should choose us

Simplifying the payment process
Our payment system enables retailers to accept online payments, providing customers with a convenient way to pay for goods and services. Customers can easily and quickly complete their purchases without the need to visit the bank or navigate complex payment methods
Increased security
Our payment system prioritizes transaction security, especially for retailers. With the use of cutting-edge data protection and encryption technology, we guarantee secure and dependable payment transactions for all
Integration with various payment gateways
Merchants can choose the most efficient and convenient payment gateway for their needs with our payment system, as it integrates with various gateways. Additionally, we offer flexible tools for managing payments and setting up payment options
Support for different currencies and payment methods
Our robust payment system provides extensive currency and payment method compatibility, enabling retailers to seamlessly transact with customers across diverse countries and regions. Additionally, we offer a wide array of payment options, encompassing bank cards, e-wallets, and mobile payments, among others
Instant confirmation of transactions
Our advanced payment system ensures real-time confirmation of transactions, delivering utmost convenience to both retailers and buyers. As soon as a buyer initiates a payment, the retailer promptly receives an instant notification, affirming the successful completion of the transaction
Flexible customisation and adaptation
Our payment system offers a comprehensive range of flexible features, empowering retailers to tailor their payment options, effectively manage transactions, configure notifications, and much more. We acknowledge the unique needs and requirements of each retailer, which is why we prioritize customization and extend dedicated support throughout the entire engagement with our payment system
